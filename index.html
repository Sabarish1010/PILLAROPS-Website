About Us

Engineering Digital Efficiency

Based in Chennai, India, PILLAROPS is an Oil & Gas Technology Solutions startup focused on automation, digital transformation, and workforce operations.

We provide scalable software solutions for oil & gas contractors, pipeline companies, industrial plants, and energy sector enterprises globally, bridging the gap between heavy industry and modern SaaS capabilities.
